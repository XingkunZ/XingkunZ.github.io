English Accumulation

Posted on 2020-08-03 In 语言 Views: Valine:

1

Tips

笔记无编排逻辑，偶尔作同义表达整理。笔记原则是“输入为了输出”。

Chunks

drop out of sth. 从…中退出

sth. 替代的位置不是只可放置名词，而是可以放置名词性质的语块，比如动词ing形式。

crouch 蹲 crouch down 趴伏 ; crouched 蜷伏的

ambitious 有野心的、有雄心的

straight A’s 全优成绩 straight-a(全优的) grade

identify 确认，鉴定

figure out sth. 搞清楚某事 figure it out

victorious 胜利的

chin 下巴

stretch out sth. 伸长，延续

积累语块时，要关注单词或短语是否即物、如何应用等性质。重点是如何应用，要以输出为目的。

Sentences

The girl once ran back home crying after a boy had hit her at school.

In the childhood, her parents encouraged her to take up anything that interested her.

As they look up, I smile and say “Happy New Year”. They smile and wish the same.

It’s a simple thing, but for a moment we have become one with the world and a sudden joy takes my breath away.

An old woman leans on a fence post(柱子，还有信件的意思). She doesn’t seem either happy or sad, just gazes into the past, or maybe the future.

具有抽象意义的名词很好用。比如描述情绪的名词，sth. comforted one’s sadness etr.

I think we’ re knocking at the door of immortality.

感悟同上。

They simply spread it out across the year. <简单地把事情分散在一年当中>

This is an example of kin election(血缘关系), a behavior common in animals in which closely related individuals take a group approach(采取团队合作的方式) to succeeding in their environment(适应环境), the researchers said.

You should never let your emotions get the better of(控制) you.

Always be willing to educate yourself(自我教育).

Pay attention and you just might make it through(撑过了…) what I call the school of life.

2

Tips

可以把doing something，something理解为具有名词性质的语块。这样的认知有助于组织出层次丰富的句子，也更好理解阅读中的长难句。

Chunks

heart-broken 伤心的

collide with 冲撞

tease 玩弄

estimate 估计，评价

grasp sth. 理解(抓住)

break into 闯入

appeal to 向…呼吁，对…有吸引力

(doing) something appeals to (doing) something

后面有例句。

bring sth. into 把…带入…

take up 占据 sth. take up my spare time

Sentences

Many people thanked Amy for being brave enough to speak up(发声).

thank sb. for (doing) something

adj. enough to do something

Amy did get her wish of a special day without teasing.

did 强调作用，的确之意

活用with/without，增加句子的层次。with/without + 名词性质的语块，eg. doing something / something，用以描述一种伴随动作。

Out came the pencil and paper and Amy went to work on her letter.

倒装。The pencil and paper came out. -> out came the pencil and paper.

At present people (who could get to that point) are not in good health at all.

定语从句

at present; be in good health

Give and you shall receive.<赠人玫瑰，手有余香。>

She thought that giving away her toys meant that she would never have any more toys.

名词性质的语块 + meant that + 句子。(意味着)

It was the season that the conscience(良心) appealed to one’ s sense of connecting with those who were less fortunate.

sense of + 名词性质的语块 (XX感，比如道德感、羞耻感；对…的直觉)

3

Tips

假定自己学英语就是为了写出一篇好英语作文——记单词就是为了使用、替换在作文里；学习句型就是为了创造一个精准又有灵气的句子。有输出才有输入。

Chunks

ignorant 无知的，愚昧的 be ignorant of (对什么不了解)

bookworm 书呆子

有些看似不像高频词的有趣词汇，会让作文很生动。

over and over 一遍一遍地 do sth. over and over

populate 居住于，移民于

dive 跳水

gap 裂缝

door、gap、bridge等词汇都很适合与抽象意义的名词合用。通往什么的道路、什么与什么的裂痕云云。

innocence 天真 innocent 天真的

insistent 坚持的 on sth./to do sth.

pass on 传递、传达

setback (n.) 挫折 复数setbacks 很好用！

worship (n./vt./vi.)祈祷、崇拜 sth. worship(~~崇拜) eg. money worship 金钱崇拜

general 将军

origin 后裔

关注已知词汇的其他含义。

Sentences

After senior high school, my education had become very narrow(窄，此处意为狭隘、局限).

There was a world of knowledge outside science and engineering, and I was tired of being ignorant of it.

be tired of +名词性质的词块；be ignorant of +名词性质的词块。

I dived into book after book. In short, the bookworm within me had awakened.

awake是个很好用的词。醒来、醒悟。

And I have this insistent need to pass on the values that I hold dear.

dear 作为n.有“亲爱的人”之义，作为adj.有“亲爱的、昂贵的”之义，作为adv.有“高价地、深切地”之义

观点、价值观可以hold(持某方观点)，可以pass on(传达)

这里的need是名词“需要”，被insistent修饰。this insistent need可替换为my insistence，但是“需要”的意思会失去。

Across the country, research shows that students from poor families fall farther behind over the summer than other students.

fall behind than … 落后于…

4

Tips

摘抄的部分来源是英文小说。小说的动作描写往往十分生动，可以积累动词、副词和一些灵活的用法。希望下次写作文可以用到这里的词汇。

Chunks

celebrity 名人

capture 复活

dramatic 戏剧的；急剧的；引人注目的；激动人心的

turn out 被证明是…；sth. turn out to be adj.

mental 精神的

wellbeing (n.)健康安乐

nutrient 营养物；营养的

dietary 饮食的；规定的食物、食谱

bless 祝福 bless you

lottery 彩票

cheek 脸颊

Sentences

This is like the Roman Colosseum, people have to fight to change their life.

fight to do, fight for sth.

A diet that’s good for your physical health(身体健康) is also good for your mental health(心理健康).

You don’t need to sit your loved ones down for a big conversation about your wellbeings. < 你不需要让你爱的人坐下来就你的幸福进行大讨论。 >

It’s part of taking charge of your wellbeing and doing what you can to stay healthy.

take charge of 负责

The day I was discharged(出院) we hugged and she gave me a kiss on the cheek.

The day I was discharged 这里是状语

She called me a week later, told me what had happened, and thanked me.

排比句很有力量，是一种简单的写长句的方式。

5

Tips

有一些 be of 名词 = be 形容词 的例子，eg. of pain = painful (痛苦的), of importance = important(重要的)，of horror = horrible(可怕的)

Chunks

proof (n.)证据;(adj.)能防护的 water-proof 防水的

declare 公布，声明 sth. is declared

dreadful 令人讨厌的

stricken 受挫的、受煎熬的

gleam 隐约闪烁

client 委托人

confine (n./vt.)限制 confine within

curve 曲线

hesitantly 犹豫地 hesitate 犹豫

endless 无尽的

imaginable 可想象

auction 拍卖

Sentences

Together we lurched(蹒跚) through the door, and an instant afterwards had thrown ourselves down upon the grass plot(草坪).

thrown ourselves down 的表达其实是说我们自己躺下

Those staring eyes and gnashing teeth flashed past us like a dreadful vision(幻象).

vision 视力、美景、幻象

gnashing teeth 咬紧的牙齿

It’s full of old houses which are named after the men who built them centuries ago.

以建造者的名字命名的房子

While they were talking, a sudden cry(尖叫) of pain was heard.

此处被动语态的使用，是因为需要突出a sudden cry。这声尖叫的微妙的突发性、自发性体现出来了，被动语态的使用场景十分值得体会。

sudden突然的，人们常常用suddenly(突然地)，其实adj.形式也很好用

With a cry of horror, he turned his wife’s face to the light and saw blood all around her lips.

The response was just as I’d expected. <这反响正如我所料。>

When I was leaving the room, I was stopped by my grandfather’s loud voice.

体会这里的被动语态，体会体会即可。

The auctioneer laid down(放下，此处译为“落下”) the gavel(木槌).

6

Tips

如果一个单词的含义十分丰富，若非兴趣驱使，不要尝试一次全部记住；或者可以只记住一个你认为最好用的意思。

Chunks

currently 当前的

role 角色、地位 play a role in 某事件/情况

propose 提议、建议 propose sb. to do

date back to 追溯到 Patents(专利) date back to Venice in the 15th century.

claim (n./v.)宣称、要求 claim that …

contrast (n./v.)对比

in contrast 与此相反。表达相反意见时的转折连接。In contrast, (I think that )…

by contrast 相比之下，很适合做句子中的插入语，eg.我不是个擅长表达的人, by contrast, 我更擅长思考。

contrast with 与…相比 The contrast with other students of Bob is sharp.(其他学生和鲍勃的区别显著)

relentless 无情的 relentless water (流水无情)

onscreen 屏幕上的

mimic (adj.)模拟的；(v.)模拟、模仿

monitor 监视器、班长(老师的人力监视器)

smoothing 宽心的

snore 打呼噜

basically 基本上、主要地

consist 由…组成；在于；符合

consist of (由…组成) sth. consist of sth. (是主动句)

consist in (在于) sth. consist in sth. (也是主动句) Love does not consist in gazing at each other.

Sentences

When the pretty woman on stage turned into a handsome young man, the audience went wild(直译为“变得疯狂”，意译为“观众沸腾了”).

The product is portable(便携的) and will switch itself off after 30 minutes to save power.

switch sth. on/off 对电器的打开与关闭

About a month later, there was a knock at the door.

注意knock的名词性，以及它依旧使用at与door连接。

He was very courageous(有胆识的) and died in battle(决斗) while rescuing another soldier.

状语从句的省略：while (he was) rescuing another soldier。可省略的原因：这句状语从句的主语与主句的主语一致，即：他在做这件事的时候，while(他在)做那件事。

7

Tips

认识一个单词的过程中，必须要学习这个单词可以以什么形式出现在你的文章中。

Chunks

agendas 议题

tough 艰难的

nature 本质；…之类的、类型 比较常见的意思是：自然

in nature 本质上；in the nature 在户外

本质上：in fact / in practice

ingredient 原材料；要素、组成部分。

wrap 包裹

condiment 调味料

flavour (n.)气味 (v.)给…调味 flavoured sth. with sth.

alphabet 字母表

peer 同龄人

muffin 松饼

cliff 悬崖

Sentences

It may be dipped in soy sauce, and may be flavoured with wasabi(山葵), which may reduce the risk of food poisoning.

dip 蘸

soy sauce 酱油；food poisoning 食物中毒(n.)

reduce the risk(风险) of…(sth.) 减少…的风险

What I’m trying to say is that you don’t have to be a blindness expert(盲人专家) right now.

主语从句

No negative peer pressure, no other children or teens making fun of your son because he’s blind.

I knew he had his feet firmly planted on the ground, and it seems that no matter what else went crazy, he would be the one constant.

had his feet firmly planted on the ground 脚踏实地

With tears streaming from my eyes, I opened the door.

(伴随状态)眼泪流出。tears streaming down like raindrops 涕零如雨

8

Tips

作文不要刻意写长难句，应注意文章节奏，短句也很有力量感。

Chunks

speechless 无言的

drumsticks 鸡腿

awkwardness 尴尬(n.)

toss 抛，投；甩(头)；掷硬币决定(v.)

trim 修剪

prayer 祈祷者

separation 分开(n.)

rumor 谣言、传闻(n.)

reveal 揭穿 阅读常见

continuously 持续不断地 continue(v.) 副词很好用

Sentences

Little did he know that she never thought he understand her at all; little did he know that she hates drumstickers even though all he wants is the best for her.

little did sb. know that 某人不知道的是…

even though 虽然/即使 注意虽然/即使在中文意思中的差别

the best for … 对某人/某事是最好的(best是名词)

Learn to love each other for who they are, not what they are.

what they are 指某人的身份

He picks up the phone, started dialing(拨号) her number. Ringing never stops. He never stop dialing.

短句的力量感。

Because you don’t know how long it will take.

take+时间长度 take your time 别着急

Friendship is like wine, it gets better as it grows older.

Love could not prevent god of death arrival.

god of death 死神 god of sth …之神

arrival 到达(n.) 很好用的词

9

Tips

注意作文每个段落的目的，使重点内容简洁明了，恰当的细节可以给应用文添彩。

Chunks

desparately 绝望地、拼命地

effort 努力(n.)

promote the exchange of … 促进…的改变

changes occur in … 变化发生在…/…在变化

keen on doing … 喜爱/热衷于做某事

open one’s mind to … 对某事敞开某人心扉

open wide the eyes of sb. 扩展某人的视野

with openness and honesty 怀着开放与诚实(的态度/原则) 可以加在句首或句末

Sentences

Later word came that <消息传来> Dolly the sheep had become seriously ill.

word(消息) came that …

With only about 1000 pandas left in the world, China is desparately trying to clone the animal and save the endangered species<濒临灭绝的物种>.

with … left, …

be desparately trying to do 竭尽全力地… try to do 尽力去做某事

I certainly appreciate their effort and there’s a lot we can learn from what they are attemping to do.

But Jules Pretty and Jo Barton, who led this study, said that until now no one knew how much time needed to be spent on green exercise.

No matter how ordinary a job is, it plays a part in society and therefore deserves our due respect.

play a part in … 在…发挥一部分作用； play a … role in … 在…中扮演…的角色

due 应有的(adj.)； deserve one’s respect 值得某人的尊重

Expert say the way you design your home could play a role in whether you pack on the pounds or keep them off.

play a role in

pack on the pounds == put on weight

10

Tips

不要刻意记短语。除了一些很特殊很固定的搭配，短语的组成其实很灵活很自由，或者有规律可循。

Chunks

habitually 惯常地 habit

outlines 概括、大纲(n.)

situation involving … 涉及…的情况(n.)

do sth. in particular to … 专门针对…做某事 In particular to sth. <特别针对…，…>, ….

avoid facing up to … 逃避面对…

altogether 总共、完全(adj. adv.) 注意拼写

a discontinued model 一个停产的型号

be forced into doing / be forced to do 被迫做某事

Sentences

The conversation is taken up<开始> with the discussion of … . <这段谈话开始于一段针对…的讨论>

take up 开始从事…; 占据时间 这句中两个意思似乎都有

I had my realatives give me money for my birthday instead of a lot of clothes that wouldn’t fit.

would 有惯常的意思

Then I started developing a problem with the set that involved static noise.

develope a new idea / product / problem / disease

Students who take a well-planned year out are more likely to be satisfied with, and complete their chosen course.

well-planned 良好计划过的

注意这句中的并列

Owain James, the president of the National Union of Students(NUS), argued that the increase is evidence of student hardship – young people are being forced into earning money before finishing their education.

argue that 主张、声称

be forced into doing sth. 被迫做某事 注意这里的 正在进行时+被动语态
